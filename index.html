How Your Therapy Journey Unfolds

Step 1: Intake & Consultation

Start with a confidential phone consultation. We learn about your needs, match you with the right provider, and verify insurance or self-pay options—all within a day or two.

Step 2: Assessment & Planning

Your therapist will conduct an in-depth assessment and collaborate with you on a custom care plan. This includes therapy format, frequency, and measurable goals.

Step 3: Consistent Sessions

Regular 45–60-minute sessions are offered weekly or bi-weekly. Your therapist adjusts approaches based on your progress and evolving goals.

Step 4: Long-Term Support

As your mental health improves, sessions may taper or evolve. Crisis plans, referrals, and support continuity ensure you always have a safety net.
